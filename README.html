ERD maps (Fig. 1) and animated maps (Fig. 2), low vs. high IQ, where

%ERD=μVR2−μVA2μVR2·100,

with alpha power μV2reference interval R and activity interval A, see Neubauer et al. (2002).

Where power Px within given frequency f, with upper alpha band between 10.70−12.69Hz in ℂ is defined by

Px(f)=ℜ(ax)2+ℑ(ax)2,

c.f. Schrausser (2000) and Schrausser and Neubauer (2024).
